### What's in the Box (Flash Fiction)

'What's in the box?'
Kate looked at her disheveled boyfriend Terrence who suddenly looked panicked, his wild brown hair vibrating as he jump up from his desk.

'Don't open it! No! Noooo!'
Terrence fell to the floor in despair 
'You have no idea what you have just done!'

He writhed in agony and screamed into Kate's shocked face as the full realisation of what she had accidentaly unleashed burned in his brain
