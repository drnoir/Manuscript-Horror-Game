# Candy Man

Battles with his mind more than anything else, the lines get blurred sometimes and lashes out, doesn’t understand his actions properly, acts emotionally.

Can be found in a cell, mumbling and groaning about nonesense (has an eery tone to it, not directly aggressive but definitely intimidating)

The player character is offered a misssble quest progression where you approach him

CM: “oi, you there, you’ve got to help me get out, I’m losing my mind in here”

### Pc options:

1. Dismiss his personal problems and agree to help (release him and he kills you)
2. Refuse to help him (quest ends, CM fate is sealed in the cell)
3. Agree to help and encourage CM to try the funky flora in his cell ( CM mutates but becomes docile, no longer plagued by the battle in his brain, does not kill you when released)(requires finding a letter from CM to a guard  mentioning how one of the prisoners had managed to stash some weed from the guard but noted how the “mould” was leeching onto it)

(CM can be heard muttering in distance and when walking past the cage, manic and frustrated statements display the turmoil he’s currently in)
